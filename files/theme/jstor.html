Vernon HS library offers college search engine


The Vernon Township High School Library/Media Center recently announced a new webpage and the addition of JSTOR, the leading research data base in the country. VTHS is the only high school in the county that offers JSTOR, giving students access to more sources when doing college prep level research papers.

Primarily in college and university libraries, JSTOR possesses thousands of credible and valid journal articles for all academic subjects. The library also offers Ebsco Host and Proquest, two excellent databases that can be found in every college library.
